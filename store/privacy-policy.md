# btrtabs — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: TODO_

btrtabs ("the extension") is designed with privacy in mind.

## Data collection

The extension does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

All data created or used by the extension (preferences, tab metadata) is
stored **locally** on your device via the `chrome.storage.local` API and
never leaves your browser.

## Permissions

- **tabs** — Used solely to read and organize the tabs in your browser.
  No tab content or URL is transmitted off-device.
- **storage** — Used solely to persist your preferences locally.

## Third parties

The extension does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or
advertising service.
